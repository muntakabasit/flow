## 🚀 Competitive Barriers

### Why Competitors Can't Copy This

1. **Privacy architecture** — Requires rethinking entire stack
2. **On-device ML** — Needs expertise in edge computing
3. **Open-source leverage** — We use free models; they'd need licensing
4. **Latency optimization** — 1000ms is hard; achieving <500ms requires mastery
5. **User experience** — Confidence scoring + speaker labels are simple but powerful
6. **Community** — Open-source community drives improvements
7. **First-mover advantage** — No one is doing this (yet)

## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Whisper model accuracy improves | LOW | Works in our favor; we get better for free |
| Larger AI companies enter | MEDIUM | We're in niche (privacy + on-device); hard to compete |
| Model licensing changes | LOW | All models are open-source; worst case: fork |
| Regulatory (GDPR, CCPA) | LOW | Our advantage (data stays local) |
